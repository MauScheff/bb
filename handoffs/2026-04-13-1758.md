## Important boundary finding

The final "no audio" bug was not a control-plane failure. The shared session state was correct, but the sender produced no real outgoing media chunks.

The working fix was in [PCMWebSocketMediaSession.swift](/Users/mau/Development/Turbo/Turbo/PCMWebSocketMediaSession.swift):

- on real transmit start, do not trust the prewarmed capture route
- explicitly restart the capture engine
- remove and reinstall the input tap
- rebuild the capture converter
- then begin sending on the live `PlayAndRecord` route

This matters because prewarm and actual PTT transmit do not necessarily use the same live route.

Healthy receiver sequence:

- `Signal received ... type=transmit-start`
- `PTT audio session activated`
- `Preparing receive media session after PTT audio activation`
- `Audio chunk received`
- `Playback buffer scheduled`
- `Playback node started`

## Current limitation

Prewarm is currently local per device, not globally synchronized. One side can become `ready` before the other if its own local warmup finishes first.

That was left as-is for now because the immediate boundary problem was "no audio on first transmit." A future refinement may gate hold-to-talk on receiver-readiness too, not just local readiness.
