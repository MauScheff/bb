## What changed this session

- Added a live-PTT background WebSocket preservation rule in `PTTViewModel`.
  - Joined-but-idle background behavior still suspends as before.
  - Live transmit / pending begin / active Apple PTT / wake flows keep or resume the WebSocket.
- Changed `BackendClient` so a normal WebSocket close schedules reconnect when `shouldMaintainWebSocket` is still true.
  - This closes the hole where a normal server close could leave the app permanently idle during a live path.
- Added `PCMWebSocketMediaSession` transport recovery:
  - A transient send failure marks media failed.
  - A later successful send reports recovery and moves media back to `connected`.
- Added Swift regressions:
  - `backgroundTransitionPreservesWebSocketDuringLiveTransmit`
  - `audioChunkSenderReportsRecoveryAfterTransientTransportFailure`

## What is not working

- Not yet proven on physical devices after the latest patch.
- The first warm hold previously worked with the conservative Apple-gated path but could feel slow; the latest trace proves at least one startup was fast, but the live background transport recovery path was broken.
- Direct QUIC may still drop under Apple/background transitions. The expected behavior after this patch is not “Direct QUIC never drops”; it is “fallback/reprobe signaling and relay audio do not become permanently stranded.”
