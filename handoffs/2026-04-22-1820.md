## What is not working

- The product model is still incomplete. The user wants to introduce a first-class `busy` state for peers who are in another live session with a third party.
- The current code implements an intermediate design (`online / live / ready / requested / offline`) but the user’s desired model is richer and action-driven.
- The next design should likely separate at least three concerns instead of compressing them:
  - peer availability / reachability
  - relationship / intent (`none`, outgoing request, incoming request)
  - current session posture (`ready`, `live`, maybe `busy`)
- The user described a stronger UX model:
  - three top-level contact buckets:
    1. normal contacts
    2. requested
    3. wants to talk
  - actions depend on both relationship and availability:
    - `request`
    - `ping`
    - `connect` or `go live`
- `busy` is not implemented yet, and its authoritative backend/source-of-truth seam still needs to be chosen deliberately.
- The selected-screen idle copy still uses `Ready to connect` for non-connected idle states. That may change once the action model is redesigned.
