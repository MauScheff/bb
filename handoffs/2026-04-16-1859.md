  - Apple then ends Blake transmit immediately after
- Strong implication:
  - this is no longer primarily a wake/audio activation bug
  - this is now a backend/control-plane lease-lifecycle bug, or a mismatch between backend lease lifetime and the client’s first-renew timing
- Open question:
  - why the backend transmit state is gone by the time the first renew completes, even after renew now starts from backend grant
