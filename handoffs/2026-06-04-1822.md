- `IncomingAudioIngressExecutor` now treats expired live backlog or expired sender-clock age as a poison for the whole `(contactID, receiveEpoch)` playback gate.
- Once an epoch is poisoned, every later same-epoch packet is rejected, including lower sequence numbers that previously looked like an unordered packet-media restart.
- Expired live-audio repair now runs even when repetitive drop diagnostics are suppressed.
- The backend-expired-lease drop path rejects media before playback and repairs local selected-conversation state.
- Focused Swift regressions now encode the observed contradiction and the receive-turn repair paths.
