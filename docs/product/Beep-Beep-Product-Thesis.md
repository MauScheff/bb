# Product Principles

## Make the First Move Easy

If you're thinking about someone, reaching out should be effortless.

## Start Small

Not every conversation needs to begin as a phone call.

Small moments often become meaningful conversations.

## Reduce Pressure

Communication should not feel like a commitment.

People should be able to reach out without wondering whether it's worth a call.

## Stay Human

Voice carries emotion, personality, context, and presence.

Protect that.

## Remove Friction

Every extra step is a conversation that never happens.

## Create Moments

The goal is not messages.

The goal is moments between people.

## Preserve Spontaneity

Some of the best conversations are unexpected.

The product should encourage natural, unplanned connection.
