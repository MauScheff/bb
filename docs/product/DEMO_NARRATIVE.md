# The Demo Narrative

Agent-facing demo script. Principle: demo a before -> after transformation, not features. Keep the story aligned with the latest product, brand, and marketing thesis in [`Beep-Beep-Product-Thesis.md`](/Users/mau/Development/bb/docs/product/Beep-Beep-Product-Thesis.md).

## The Setup (10-15 seconds)

Start with something painfully relatable:

> "Let me show you something everyone here has done this week."

Then:

> "You want to quickly talk to someone, but you don't want to call."

Pause.

> "So you do this..."

## The Before

Story:

> "You send a voice note..."
>
> "They reply 5 minutes later..."
>
> "You send another..."
>
> "Now it's 6 messages back and forth..."

Then land it:

> "What should've been a 30-second conversation turns into 10 minutes."

Let that sink in.

## Transition

> "So we built a way to just... talk."

Short. Confident.

## The After

Now demo your product:

> "I tap once. I signal I want to talk."
>
> "They see it, and join when they're ready."
>
> "And now we're in a conversation."

Explain lightly as it happens:

- No interruption
- No pressure
- One person speaks at a time

## Highlight the Magic

Call out what just happened:

- No missed call.
- No back-and-forth messages.
- No talking over each other.

Then:

> "It's just a simple, clear conversation."

## The Insight

Give them the mental hook:

> "People don't avoid talking. They avoid the pressure of calling."
>
> "This removes that completely."

## The Payoff

Bring it back to value:

> "What used to take 10 minutes now takes 30 seconds."

## The Close

End clean:

> "Start a conversation, without calling."

Stop talking.

## Full Script (Compressed Version)

If you had 60 seconds:

> "Let me show you something everyone here has done.
>
> You want to talk to someone, but you don't want to call.
>
> So you send a voice note... they reply later... you send another...
>
> What should be a 30-second conversation takes 10 minutes.
>
> We built a way to just talk.
>
> I tap once, they join when ready, and now we're in a conversation.
>
> No interruption, no pressure, no overlap, just clear communication.
>
> People don't avoid talking. They avoid the pressure of calling.
>
> This removes that.
>
> Start a conversation, without calling."

## Why This Works

- Starts with shared pain
- Shows behavior, not features
- Creates a clear before/after contrast
- Anchors on a simple insight
- Ends with a memorable line

## Common Mistakes

Avoid these:

- Explaining PTT technically
- Talking about frameworks or infrastructure
- Showing too many screens
- Letting the demo drag

If it takes more than about 60-90 seconds, you lose them.

## Pro Move

If you can pull it off, do it live with another person:

- Send request
- They accept
- Exchange 1-2 lines

That moment:

> "oh, that's actually smooth"

is everything.

## Final Truth

Your product is not obvious on slides.

But in a demo, it can become instantly obvious.

That's your leverage.
