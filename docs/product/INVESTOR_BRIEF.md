## The Opportunity

People communicate more than ever, but starting a conversation is harder.

- Calls are avoided due to social pressure.
- Messaging is flexible but slow and fragmented.
- Voice notes are growing, but inefficient.

Missing layer: something between messaging and calling.

## The Solution

Conversation flow:

- Signal you want to talk.
- The other person joins when ready.
- You connect in a simple, structured voice interaction.

Positioning: start a conversation without calling.

## Product

A lightweight communication app designed for clarity, simplicity, and reliability.

- No threads, no typing, no noise.
- One interaction, one conversation.
- Turn-based voice, no overlap, no chaos.

Tagline: easy to start, easy to talk.

## Value Proposition

We combine what existing tools cannot:

| | Messaging | Calls | Our Product |
| --- | --- | --- | --- |
| Real conversation | ❌ | ✅ | ✅ |
| Low pressure | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Flexible timing | ✅ | ❌ | ✅ |
| Clarity | ❌ | ❌ | ✅ |

Promise: instant, clear conversations on your terms.

## Why Now

- Calls are declining because of social friction.
- Voice usage is rising through voice notes and audio.
- Real-time infrastructure is now seamless and reliable.
- Users are overwhelmed by messaging overload.

Behavior and technology are aligned for a new communication layer.

## Initial Market

### Early Adopters

- Young professionals (20-40)
- Couples, close friends, and small teams
- Heavy users of voice notes

### Use Cases

- Quick coordination
- Replacing voice note back-and-forth
- Short, real conversations without calling

## Go-To-Market

Bottom-up, network-driven growth.

- Paired onboarding creates value in the first interaction.
- Each conversation requires another user.
- Organic spread happens through existing relationships.

### Entry Wedge

> Stop sending multiple voice notes. Just talk.

## Retention

Turbo becomes the default for:

- Quick conversations
- Situations where messaging is too slow
- Situations where calling feels too heavy

It complements messaging instead of replacing it.

## Product Principles

- **Reliable:** works instantly, every time
- **Simple:** no learning curve
- **Non-intrusive:** respects attention
- **Structured:** clear, turn-based interaction

It should feel like infrastructure, not another app.

## Vision

To become the default way people initiate conversations, sitting between messaging and calling as a new standard layer.

## Insight

People do not avoid talking. They avoid the cost of initiating a call.

**Turbo removes that cost.**

## Why This Wins

- Introduces a new behavior, not just a better feature
- Solves a universal, daily problem
- Built around natural network effects
- Simple enough to become a default habit

## Closing Line

**We’re not improving communication tools. We’re redefining how conversations start.**
